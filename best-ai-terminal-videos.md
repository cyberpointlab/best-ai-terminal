## The Tools Covered

1. **Claude Code** (Anthropic) — Agent-first CLI tool; 200K token context; dominant terminal AI coding tool as of early 2026.
2. **Aider** (Open source) — Git-native AI pair programming; works with Claude, GPT-4o, DeepSeek, local models; free and open-source.
3. **Gemini CLI** (Google) — Free tier with generous limits; open-source; good entry point.
4. **OpenCode** — Open-source Go-based TUI; supports 75+ LLM providers including local models; LSP support.
5. **Codebuff** — Terminal-first assistant for repo-level changes.
6. **GitHub Copilot CLI** — Extension of Copilot into the terminal.
